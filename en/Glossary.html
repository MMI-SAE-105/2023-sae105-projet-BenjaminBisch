GLOSSARY

Glossary

Poudlard: The iconic wizarding school where Harry Potter and his friends study magic.

Horcruxes: Magical objects in which Voldemort hid a portion of his soul to become immortal.

Death Eaters: The followers of Lord Voldemort, responsible for many evil acts in the series.

Dobby: The brave house-elf who aspires to freedom, a memorable character in the saga.

Voldemort: The main evil sorcerer, Harry Potter's enemy.

Deathly Hallows: Three legendary objects believed to make the possessor invincible.

Hedwig's Theme: The iconic musical melody of the series, associated with the magical world of Harry Potter.

Dursley: The family with whom Harry lives at the beginning of his life before discovering his status as a wizard.

Resurrection Stone, Elder Wand, and Invisibility Cloak: The three Deathly Hallows.
